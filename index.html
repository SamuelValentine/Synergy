Welcome to Synergy!

Synergy is a youth ministry network based at Grace Church in Verdun.

Our mission statement: To provide a secure and caring Christian community to equip youth to grow in their relationship with Christ.

Youth group takes place weekly from 5:00 to 7:00 PM on Sundays. Typically, this time is filled with games, snacks, and biblical learning.

This is a time for any teens or young adults to come down and simply enjoy the company of others their age. You can bring your homework, book, or laptop and work alongside your friends!

This event is hosted by Joel O'Kill on Wednesdays from 3:30 - 5:30 PM. All 6th graders and over are welcome to come hang out. :)

Come out every Sunday from 5-7 pm to our Youthgroup event at Grace Church in Verdun every Sunday! Our typical evening includes a mix of games, snacks, teaching, and small groups. Bring a friend and come enjoy some great company before heading back to school for the week!

All teens are welcome, aged 12 - 17.
